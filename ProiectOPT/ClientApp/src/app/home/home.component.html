"Do it bigger"

This is our home, the Smoothie King Center. The arena was completed in 1999 at a cost of $114 million and officially opened on October 19, 1999. the arena seats 17,805 for concerts, 16,867 for Pelicans games, 18,500 for college basketball and Pelicans playoff games, and 16,900 for ice hockey and arena football. It has 2,800 club seats and 56 luxury suites. the arena as a concert venue can seat 7,500 for half-stage shows, 17,221 for end-stage shows and 17,805 for a center-stage shows. For trade shows and conventions the arena features 17,000 square feet (1,600 m2) of space. the ceiling is 65 feet (20 m) to beam and roof, 70 feet (21.5 m) to the top of the arena.
